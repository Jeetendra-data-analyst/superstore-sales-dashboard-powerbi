# 🛒 Superstore Sales Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📊 Project Overview
This project presents an interactive Sales Analysis Dashboard developed using the Global Superstore dataset.  
It provides a comprehensive view of sales performance, profitability, and regional trends through intuitive and visually engaging visualizations.  

The dashboard transforms raw data into meaningful insights, helping understand business performance and support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objective
The objective of this project is to analyze retail sales data and identify:
- Sales and profit trends  
- High-performing regions and categories  
- Areas with low profitability  
- Impact of discounts on profit  

---

## 📁 Dataset
- Source: Global Superstore Dataset  
- Features include:
  - Region & State  
  - Category & Sub-category  
  - Sales, Profit & Quantity  
  - Order Date & Customer Segment  

---

## ⚙️ Tools Used
- Power BI (Dashboard & Visualization)  
- Excel (Data Cleaning & Pre-processing)  
- DAX (Calculated Measures & KPIs)  

---

## 📈 Key Insights
- Some regions generate high sales but lower profit margins  
- Certain categories consistently perform better than others  
- Seasonal trends impact overall sales performance  
- Discounts have a direct impact on profitability  

---

## 📊 Business Impact
This dashboard helps analyze sales and profit performance across regions and categories, enabling better decision-making and identification of growth opportunities.
